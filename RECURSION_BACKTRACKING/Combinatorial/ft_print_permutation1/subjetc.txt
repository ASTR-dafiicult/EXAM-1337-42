file : ft_allsec_alpha.c;

<-------------------------------------->
Given an alphabet {a, b} of length k
generate all sequences from aaa to bbb in lexicographical order.
print the tracing table for all sequences.

<EXAMPLE>
---------

k = 3

<OUTPUT>
---------
a  a  a
a  a  b
a  b  a
a  b  b
b  a  a
b  a  b
b  b  a
b  b  b
